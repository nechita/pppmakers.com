Hi, my name is Gabriel, and I'm an aspiring FullStack Developer from Romania.

I love to learn new stuff, and in my search for the best learning material I end up on a lot of premium courses landing pages.

Most of these courses have fair prices, but usually the pricing is tailored for people in more affluent countries from North America, Western & Northen Europe and a few other countries scattered accross the globe.

My country is anything but affluent, so the financial strain to educate myself by using the best resources on the web is quit big.

Thanks to the makers & businesses you will find on this website, me and other curious minds from around the world benefit from location-based discounts, that make it more affordable for us to learn and create stuff.
