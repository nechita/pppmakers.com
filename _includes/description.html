What is PPPMakers?

We feature courses or products discounted according to the customer’s Purchasing Power Parity. Part of your audience might live in a country which isn't a great global purchasing power.

Why would I discount my products?

People who can’t afford to pay for products might end up getting a copy by other means, which can translate for you in lost sales. As such, we're trying to help makers expand their customer base by adopting a new pricing strategy.

How do I get my product featured?

Looking to have your product featured on PPPMakers? Drop us a tweet/DM on (@NechitaGabriel) with your product and a brief description and we'll get back to you ASAP.
