What is PPPMakers?
We'll feature courses or products discounted based on the customer’s Purchasing Power Parity. Part of your audience might live in a country which isn't a great global purchasing power.

Why would I discount my courses?
People who can’t afford to pay for courses will end up getting a copy by other means...which is a loss for everyone involved. As such, we're trying to help course makers expand their customer base by adopting a new pricing strategy.

How do I get my course featured?
Looking to have your course featured on PPPMakers? Drop us a tweet/DM on (@NechitaGabriel) with your course and a brief description and we'll get back to you asap.
